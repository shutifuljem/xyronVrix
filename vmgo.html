Vision

To be progressive and peaceful Barangay. To provide and deliver public service through honesty, integrity, and God-fearing anchored by the unity solidarity and teamwork.

Mission

To provide and deliver dedicated public service where status of life, belief, religions are respected and honored to all constituents.

Goal

To become one of the Most Outstanding Barangay all year through with the support of brave and competent officials and with the unending support of constituents.
